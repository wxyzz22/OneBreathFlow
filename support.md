## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is OneBreathFlow?

OneBreathFlow is a guided breathing app that helps you relax, focus, boost energy, and improve sleep. A glowing orb on screen guides each inhale, hold, and exhale so you can breathe without counting.

### Where are the main pages in the App?

The OneBreathFlow app is designed to have a "single-page" experience. In the main page of the app, you can find 4 main buttons:

- Top Left corner button: <u>Settings</u> page
- Top Right corner button: <u>Progress Insight</u> page
- Bottom button (displaying selected breathing exercise): <u>Session Configuration</u> page
- Center glowing orb button: <u>start a Breathing Session</u>

### How do I start a breathing session?

Simply open OneBreathFlow on your device and tap the breathing orb to begin.

### What are the four breathing modes?

- **Calm** — ease stress and quiet your mind  
- **Focus** — center yourself before work or study  
- **Energy** — feel refreshed and alert  
- **Relief** — release tension and reset  

### How do I customize my session?

From the session configuration screen, you can adjust intensity, duration, and exercise. Premium users can access deeper customization, including detailed breathing patterns, custom sound and haptic cues, background sounds, and custom themes.

A general pattern for customization is as follows: 

- Tap the button below the breathing orb showing the selected breathing exercise name to open the <u>Session Configuration</u> area.
- Make quick customization by choosing a mode (Calm, Focus, Energy, or Relief), intensity, and duration for the breathing session.
- You can also turn off sound and visual cues during the active breathing session.
- Make more personalized choices by clicking "Customize" (end of page) to land in **Premium Plan section**: change default exercise for each mode, create your own exercise, and configure visual guidance, sound and haptic experience.

### Can I save my customized session?

Yes! In the <u>Session Configuration</u> area (open by tapping the button below the breathing orb), you can configure your preferred breathing session settings, and "Save" it by tapping the button to the top-right corner (a button with arrown-downward icon). 

You can quickly load any previously saved session configuration from the top-left corner button (a button with folder icon).

Your most recently used session configuration settings will be the default for next breathing session.

### Does OneBreathFlow work with Apple Watch?

Yes. OneBreathFlow includes an Apple Watch companion app with haptic guidance. Make sure the Watch app is installed and your devices are paired and connected.

### How to Add Exercise to Apple Watch?

In the <u>Session Configuration</u> area (open by tapping the button below the breathing orb), you can configure your preferred breathing session settings, and "Save" the breating session setting to Apple Watch by tapping the button to the top-right corner (a button with arrown-downward icon). Make sure to check the box for "Save to Apple Watch".

You can also manage your saved session settings via the top-left corner button (a button with folder icon).

### What is OneBreathFlow Pro?

OneBreathFlow Pro is an optional premium subscription that unlocks:

- Custom sound and haptic cues
- Fully tailored breathing sessions
- Unlimited theme customization
- Calming background sounds

... all future premium features updates.

### How do I subscribe to OneBreathFlow Pro?

Open **Settings** in the app and tap **Upgrade**, or tap a premium feature (such as **Customize**, **Custom Designs**, or **Background Sound**) to view the subscription options.

### How do I restore my purchase?

1. Open the paywall in the app (See "How do I subscribe to OneBreathFlow Pro?") 
2. Tap **Restore Purchases**.  

If you still do not see premium access, contact us at [act2wxy@gmail.com](mailto:act2wxy@gmail.com?subject=OneBreathFlow%20Restore%20Purchase) with the Apple ID used for the purchase.

### How do I cancel or manage my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through Apple, not directly in the app.

1. Open **Settings** on your iPhone.
2. Tap your **Apple ID** at the top.
3. Tap **Subscriptions**.
4. Select **OneBreathFlow** / **OneBreathFlow Pro**.  
5. Tap **Cancel Subscription** or change your plan.

You will keep premium access until the end of the current billing period.

### How do I set daily reminders?

Go to **Settings → Reminders** to add a reminder for the days and times you want to practice, or manage any existing reminders.

### Is my data private?

Yes. Your breathing sessions, streaks, themes, and settings are stored locally on your device. We do not require an account. Purchase information is handled by Apple and RevenueCat for subscription management only.

Read our full [Privacy Policy](https://wxyzz22.github.io/OneBreathFlow/).

## Troubleshooting

### The app won't start a session

- Make sure your iPhone is not in Low Power Mode if haptics or sounds seem delayed.  
- Force quit and reopen the app.  
- Restart your device.

### Apple Watch sessions are not syncing

- Confirm your Watch is paired and unlocked.  
- Open the OneBreathFlow Watch app at least once after installing.  
- Check that Bluetooth is enabled on both devices.

### Premium features are still locked after purchase

- Tap **Restore Purchases** on the paywall.  
- Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID used for the purchase.  
- If the issue continues, email us at [act2wxy@gmail.com](mailto:act2wxy@gmail.com?subject=OneBreathFlow%20Premium%20Access%20Issue).

### Notifications are not appearing

- Go to **iPhone Settings → OneBreathFlow → Notifications** and ensure notifications are allowed.  
- Confirm reminders are enabled inside the app under **Settings → Reminders**.
